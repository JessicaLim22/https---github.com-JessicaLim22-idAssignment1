Information about Me!

Hi, my name is Jessica Lim. Currently a student studying immersive media at Ngee Ann Polytechnic.
Recently graduated from ITE College West with a Higher Nitec certificate of IT Application Development.

My hobbies are playing sports, watching movies and animes. I played Netball for 9 years from Primary 3 to ITE year 1. During my secondary school days, I was the captain and vice-captain for the school team, we took part and won many competitions, I was also chosen to go for a trail run to play for the Singapore National Team. I have a passion for Netball because it was the sport that captured my eyes when I was young.
